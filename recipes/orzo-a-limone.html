Orzo A Limone

This is one of our favorite weeknight meals! Enjoy

Ingredients

  • 4 T Buter
  • 1 Lemon
  • 8 oz. Orzo
  • Sm Yellow Onion (Or a shallot), diced
  • 4-5 oz. Pancetta/Guanciale
  • 1.2 C. Frozen Peas
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt, Black Pepper, Red pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. Cook Pancetta/Guanciale over medium in a small skillet until browned. Once cooked transfer with a slotted spoon to a paper towel lined plate and set aside.
  2. Meanwhile, peel 3 strips from the lemon, zest the rest with a Microplane, then juice the lemon. Combine the zest and juice, place the strips with 1-2T of pepper, 1/2 t of red pepper flakes.
  3. Place 2T butter & 1T olive oil in a medium pot over medium-low until melted. Add the diced onion and saute until translucent but not browned.
  4. Add the lemon strips/pepper as well as a large pinch of salt. Cook for about a minute.
  5. Add the orzo and cook, stirring often, for 2-4 minutes. Don't rush this but watch for burning
  6. Add 1 1/2C of water and bring to a simmer. Cook, stirring/scraping often until the orzo is cooked and a nice sauce has developed.
  7. Run the peas under hot water in a colander to remove frost, add to pan in the last 2 minutes.
  8. Add remaining 2T butter, remove from heat and stir to combine. Slowly add grated parm until it is melted in and the mixture seems a bit too thick.
  9. Stir in the lemon juice, salt to taste.
  10. Serve with pancetta, more parm, more oil and some salt/pepper!
