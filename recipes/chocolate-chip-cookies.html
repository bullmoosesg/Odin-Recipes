Chocolate Chip Cookies

Cookie Monster would go into relapse for these bad Larry's

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Stick Butter
  • 1 Egg
  • 3/4 C White Sugar
  • 3/4 C Dark Brwon Sugar
  • 2 C Flour
  • 1 1/2 t Baking Soda
  • 1 T Salt
  • 1 T Vanilla Extract
  • 10 oz Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F and line a baking sheet with parchment.
  2. Cream the butter and sugars until well combined.
  3. Add vanilla, eggs and beat until ribbons form.
  4. Mix in dry ingredients by hand until just combined (streaks of flower are ok)
  5. Fold in chocolate chips
  6. Roll into golf-ball sized lumps and space in a 4:3:4 order on your sheets
  7. Bake until browned around the sides and lightly cracked on top, 8-10 mins
